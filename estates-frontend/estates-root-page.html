About Us

Estates Lookout is a platform developed by Inspitium Technology Company. The aim for Estates Lookout is to facilitate agents and developers alike to showcase their property listings, manage their team and manage their customers. What makes us different from others is that the platform you host from us will be all yours to manage.

Benefits of using Estates Lookout

Became a client resource:

Other than showing your services and product. You need to use your website, blog and social media to become a valuable resource with helpful information.

Brand yourself:

Set yourself apart from the competition, you do not want to be just in another standard real estates company platform. By having your own website you are creating your online brand, as your website is a direct reflection of your authority, goals and services. Home buyer and seller want to work with someone they can trust. By having your own website you can build trust from there by talking about yourself, your lifestyle, services and how you can help them.

Stay current:

Most of the buyer when searching for property, most of them will search through the browser not social media app. Buyer won’t purposely go to that company and view, but they will do their survey online. The internet makes it easier for potential clients to find you and your properties. Client are able to get in touch with you and the information you have at anytime and anywhere

Grow your personal brand:

You may argue that you do not need the added expenses of a website since your company supplies you with one as part of employment. What happen if you leave that company? Personal branding in real estates is important. You need to set yourself apart from the competition. You don’t want to the the same as everyone else in the office on some generic corporate website, otherwise what’s the benefit of the prospect choosing you over another agent in your office using the very same corporate recourse and information.

Networking through blog:

Creating a blog on your website could help you attract an audience. This would be a positive way to demonstrate your knowledge and build trust for your reader. With an active blog clients can see how you want to connect with them.

Having your own website

If there is any lead or inquiry it is all direct to you not through a company portal where the lead is distributed. All the lead are organic and you can be sure they are interested in your listing. You can be sure it is not your friend or your family member liking your website and etc.
